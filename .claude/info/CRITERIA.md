# Judging Criteria

## Creativity (25%)

- Is the problem definition or solution differentiated from existing approaches?
- Is the problem background well-motivated and credible?
- Does it present a genuinely new idea, or a clear improvement over existing solutions?

## Snowflake Expertise (25%)

- Are Snowflake platform features used appropriately and at a high level of sophistication? (optimization, not just basic usage)
- Does the solution present an innovative way of leveraging Snowflake's data assets?
- Is the solution only viable because of Snowflake? (i.e., Snowflake is essential, not incidental)

## AI Expertise (25%)

- Is Snowflake Cortex used for the right purpose and in a meaningful way?
- Is AI/agents used to create new value, not just as a surface-level feature?
- Does the solution have scalability that keeps pace with AI model advancement?

## Feasibility (15%)

- How complete and polished is the implementation?
- Is the solution cost-efficient and sustainable in the long term?
- Is the problem-solving approach logical and realistically implementable?

## Presentation & Storytelling (10%)

- Does the demo go beyond the problem statement to show innovative and creative functionality?
- Are the challenge and future roadmap clearly articulated?
- Is the demo convincing, and does Snowflake's role come through as central to the solution?
